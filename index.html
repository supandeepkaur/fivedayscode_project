How to make coffee.

Coffee can improve energy levels and make you smarter.It can help you burn fat and can drastically improve physical performance.It may lower your risk of type 2 diabetes.

Ingredients

  • Hot water
  • Coffee
  • Sugar(optional)
  • Milk or creamer (optional)
  • Cocoa, spices, or vanilla extract (optional)

Preparation

  1. To heat a cup of water quickly and easily, microwave it for 1 minute. You could also heat it on the stovetop using a pot or kettle. Heat it over medium-high heat, then take the water off of the stove just before it starts to boil.
  2. For 1 serving, heat 1 cup (240 mL) of water. Use more water if you want to make additional servings.
  3. Using a kettle will make it easier to pour the hot water into your mug.
  4. Check your instant coffee container’s label to see how much you should use to get the best taste. Most companies recommend using 1 to 2 teaspoons for 1 cup (240 mL) of water
  5. Mixing the coffee with a bit of cold water gently dissolves it. Gently dissolving the coffee instead of shocking it with hot water will improve the flavor
  6. For a richer coffee flavor, add sugar or spices after mixing the coffee with hot water. If you’d like, add a teaspoon of sugar, cocoa powder, cinnamon, or allspice
  7. Spoon dairy milk or another non-dairy option, cream, or flavored creamer into your coffee. The right amount to use depends on how light or dark you like your coffee.
  8. Give the coffee a good stir before enjoying it or serving it to someone else. Mix it until it’s uniform in color to evenly blend the milk and sugar (if you’re using them).
